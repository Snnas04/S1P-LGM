The 6 Hours of Portimão is an endurance race for sports cars held at Algarve International Circuit in Portugal.

The Algarve International Circuit was constructed in 2008 and has hosted European Le Mans Series races before with the 4 Hours of Portimão. In January 2021, it was announced that the track would host the opening round of the 2021 FIA World Endurance Championship.
